Missing the Past

He didn't stay in Smallville.
Fact 2: He kept a secret from the twins.
He didn't tell his sons the truth.

Discuss:

What happens to the verbs "stay" and "tell" when we use "didn't"?

Grammar 3

The Negative Past

To say "NO" in the past, we use didn't.

The rule is:
Subject + didn't + Normal Verb.
(The verb goes back to normal! No "ED", no irregular forms).

Positive

"He stayed."

Negative

"He didn't stay."
